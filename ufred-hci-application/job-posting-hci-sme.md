## The Opportunity

UFred is seeking a Subject Matter Expert to develop weekly learning content for CPSC 2050 Human-Computer Interaction, an undergraduate course in their fully online Bachelor of Computer Science program.

The course outline, learning outcomes, broad weekly structure, and assessment direction are already established. This contract is focused primarily on developing clear, accurate, student-friendly weekly learning materials within that existing structure.

CPSC 2050 covers: how people engage with technology, how user needs shape interface design, usability, accessibility, accountability, inclusion, responsible communication of information, graphics, interaction techniques, human vision, and visualization.
